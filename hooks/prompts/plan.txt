## Review Criteria

### 1. YAGNI (You Aren't Gonna Need It)
- Are there any unnecessary tasks included for current requirements?
- Is there excessive abstraction or design for the future?
- Are there features included in the plan that aren't immediately needed?

### 2. Completeness
- Are all steps included to meet the requirements?
- Are there any important missing steps?
- Is the dependency order correct?

### 3. Risk
- What items have technical risk?
- Which items could take significant time?
- What areas have high uncertainty?

### 4. Architecture Consistency
- Is it consistent with existing system architecture?
- Does it follow project conventions?
